What are Self Driving Cars?

Self driving cars, also known as autonomous vehicles, use AI, sensors, and advanced algorithms to navigate roads without human intervention.

These vehicles aim to improve safety, reduce traffic, and provide mobility for those unable to drive.

Key Technologies

  • Lidar and Radar Sensors
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning
  • Real-time Mapping and GPS

Benefits and Challenges

Autonomous cars promise safer roads and less congestion but face challenges such as legal regulations, ethical decisions, and technology limitations.

Ongoing research and testing continue to bring fully autonomous vehicles closer to widespread adoption.
