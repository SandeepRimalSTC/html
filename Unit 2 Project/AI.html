What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence — such as understanding language, recognizing images, and making decisions.

You may have seen AI in voice assistants, chatbots, or even video recommendations. These tools learn from data and improve over time.

Examples of AI

  • Google Assistant
  • Face Recognition
  • Chatbots

The Future of AI

AI is growing rapidly across industries like healthcare, finance, and transportation. It helps solve big problems, but also brings challenges like bias and job loss.

Experts agree: AI must be used responsibly — with fairness, safety, and transparency.
