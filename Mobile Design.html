About HighVoltage Esports Nepal

HighVoltage Esports Nepal is a competitive PUBG Mobile team that has emerged as a strong contender in the South Asian esports scene. Originally founded in Nepal, the organization now competes from Mongolia, allowing it to participate in a broader range of international tournaments.

Known for disciplined gameplay, tactical rotations, and aggressive zone control, the team has earned respect across the region with total earnings nearing $78,000.

Current Roster

  • BURNT
  • KYRO
  • Moohoo
  • SENOR
  • ZAKEE

All players joined the team in early 2024 and have competed in major tournaments like:

  • PUBG Mobile Super League South Asia Qualifier
  • PMPL South Asia
  • Ruthless Esports Invitational

Notable finishes include 11th place in PMPL South Asia Spring 2023 and 5th in Ruthless Esports Invitational.
